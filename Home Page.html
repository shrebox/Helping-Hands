About

Ever felt a sudden urge to donate for the needy but were at a loss where and how to go about it. Scared that the organisation you donate to will not use it for the right purpose?

This is the right place for you. We keep record of all the authorised NGOs and Non profit organisations. You can also digitally keep track of what is being made of the money you donated or who is using lets say the clothes you gave away.
